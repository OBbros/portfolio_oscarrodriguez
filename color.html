In this assignment, I was tasked with creating a unique color scheme for specific fictitious clients. Each color scheme was carefully selected to be well-suited to the needs, purpose, and identity of each client. I have discussed why I chose each color scheme below each image.

Kindergarten Teacher

Explanation

I chose this tetradic color scheme for the kindergarten teacher because it is bright and cheery, since kindergarten teachers often convey a sense of excitement for learning to the young students they teach. Additionally, I like how each main component of the color scheme well represents some of the primary and secondary colors on a color wheel. I would imagine that kindergarten students will learn to name these colors as part of the kindergarten curriculum.
